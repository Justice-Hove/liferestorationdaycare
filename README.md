1. ## Project Overview
This project entails the design and development of a professional, fully functional, responsive, and SEO-optimized website for Life Restoration Day Care. As a community-based Non-Profit Organization (NPO),

The success of the project is going to be achieved through research of the organisation, conducting interviews with the staff of the day care on the 15th of April to get the required assets for the website and also the local community for more information and credibility assessment of the day care.

 The website will serve to increase credibility, market the day care's English-medium curriculum, and attract potential donations. In doing so, this will then create an online presence for Life restoration day care which will act as a strong form of advertisement, marketing and an opportunity to be known on a global scale

2. ## Website Goals and Objectives
●	Attracting Donations: Act as a motivational platform to inspire financial support.
●	Creating Awareness: Inform the broader community about the NPO's existence.
●	Highlighting Activities: Showcase the daily schedule and after-school programs.
●	Increasing Credibility: Build trust through a professional digital presence.
●	Marketing: Drive enrolment inquiries for the ECD programs

4.	## Proposed website features and functionality:
I.	Home
The function is to create awareness and provide a first impression to users. 
Features:
•	Hero section
•	Mission statement
•	Navigation panel
II.	About us
Its purpose is to increase credibility by explaining the history.
Features:
•	Organization history
•	The staff
•	values
III.	Services
This section highlights the activities that happen at the day care.
Features:
•	Daily schedules
•	Curriculum overview
•	Extra curriculum
IV.	Contact
The purpose of the tab is to attract donations and enrollment leads via the details provided.
Features:
•	Contact information
•	Social media links
V.	Enquiry 
The purpose is to allow users to enquire about the day care and donations. 
Features:
•	Forms to send messages and donate to the day care
